No members of the public will be allowed in the BOCC Chambers due to concerns for public safety resulting from the 
COVID-19 emergency and pursuant to the Governor of Nevada’s Declaration of Emergency Directive 006 Section 1 which 
suspends the requirement in NRS 241.023(1)(b) that there be a physical location designated for meetings of public 
bodies where members of the public are permitted to attend and participate. This meeting will be held by 
teleconference only. 
11:00 A.M. – OPEN MEETING 
 
1. 
Public participation (no action will be taken) 
 
2. 
For Possible Action:  Approve bills; financial report 
 
3. 
For Possible Action: Approve minutes of March 18, 2020 
 
4. 
For Possible Action: Request to approve the 2019 Lyon County Chip Seal and Slurry 
Seal project in the Mason Valley area. This project will consist of roughly 9235 
square yards of slurry seal in the curb and gutter areas and 888,425 square yards 
of chip seal. It will also include Crack Sealing and striping. This project is 
estimated to cost $2,704,940.00 including bid preparations, testing and 
inspections.  Request to also add a 10% contingency to cover any unforeseen 
circumstances.  
 
5. 
For Possible Action: Request to approve funding in the amount of $1,740,000 for 
design and construction of the City of Fernley Cottonwood Lane Drainage and 
Roadway Reconstruction Project (Project).  This Project will address existing 
drainage issues, and reconstruction of pavement sections on Cottonwood LN, 
between Cook WY and Bardot WY and between Hardie LN and US-50A.  Design of 
the Project commenced in FY 19/20; the City’s Engineering Services Consultant for 
the Project, Lumos and Associates, Inc. (Lumos), has submitted the 60% Plan Set 
and Estimate of Construction Costs to the City.  Staff anticipates completion of 
design and Advertising for Construction Services in FY 20/21. 
 
6. 
For Possible Action: Request to approve funding in the amount of $260,000 for 
Engineering Design Services of the City of Fernley’s Shadow LN Reconstruction 
Project (Project).  This Project will address existing drainage issues, and 
reconstruction of pavement sections on Shadow LN, between US-50A and SR 828 
(Farm District Road).  Sanitary sewer and water infrastructure will be evaluated for 
replacement and/or upsizing within the Project area.
