3.1 
Discussion on recent and upcoming Special Events. 
 
Park & Recreation Dir. McGuire reported the Fishing Derby will be held on 
September 19, 2020.  Ten Acre Lake will be stocked in June as well.  A 
carnival and a car show will be held at the 4th of July event this year. 
 
3.2 
Discussion on Park Improvements.

 Splash Pads – Have been repainted and resurfaced and are scheduled 
to open on May 22, 2020. 
 Trails – Trail maps are expected to be up by the end of the week. 
 Parks – Restrooms re-open on Friday.  Playgrounds have been opened 
and are being sanitized twice a day. 
 Budget Cuts – Due to budget cuts all capital items have been deferred 
to at least December 2020. 
 
3.3 
Discussion on Committees. 
 
 Running Group – Still ongoing.  There has been limited participation.  
Runs are still occurring twice a week. 
 
3.4 
Discussion on Sports Programming. 
 
 
Park & Recreation Director McGuire reported Bouse is now open for 
tournaments.  There are sanitation stations distributed throughout the 
facility.  The City leagues open on May 18, 2020.  There are currently 24 
teams enrolled.  The Golf Course opened on May 1 and is going well.
