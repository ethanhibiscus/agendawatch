CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PLAN COMMITTEE

In accordance with the order of the office of the Governor issued March 16, 2020.Notice 
is hereby given that a meeting of the Capital Improvement Plan Committee of the City 
of Marble Falls, Texas will be held Thursday, June 11 at 6:00 pm in the Lakeside 
Pavilion located at 307 Buena Vista, Marble Falls, Texas, and telephonic 
participation in order to advance the public health goal of limiting face-to-face meetings 
(also called “social distancing”) to slow the spread of the Coronavirus (COVID-19).  
 
The public dial-in number to participate in the meeting is 1-346-248-7799 
 
The meeting ID is 951 5815 4818 
 
The public will be permitted to offer public comments as provided by the agenda and as 
permitted by the presiding officer during the meeting. A recording of the telephonic 
meeting will be made and will be available to the public in accordance with the Open 
Meetings Act upon written request. 
 
This notice is posted pursuant to the Texas Government Code, Chapter §551-Open 
Meetings. 
 
1. CALL TO ORDER. 
2. APPROVAL OF MINUTES:  Minutes from the regular meeting on June 19, 2019 
3. PRESENTATION: Update on CIP Program and grant awards 
4. DISCUSSION: Review of Proposed 2020-2024 Capital Improvement Plan 
5. DISCUSSION and RECOMMENDATION: Regarding the 2020-2024 Capital 
Improvement Plan to be presented to City Council for the FY 20-21 budget. 
 
6. ADJOURNMENT.
