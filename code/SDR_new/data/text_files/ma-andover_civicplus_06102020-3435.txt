 
Bancroft Elementary School 
Council  
Agenda June 10, 2020 
Location: Virtual Meeting via Zoom 
 Time: 9:00 AM 
 
 
1. Welcome 
2. Discussion on School Improvement Plan 
 
Pursuant to Governor Baker’s March 12, 2020 Order Suspending Certain Provisions of the Open 
Meeting Law, and the Governor’s March 15, 2020 Order imposing strict limitation on the number of 
people that may gather in one place, this meeting of the Bancroft Elementary School Council Meeting 
will be conducted via remote participation to the greatest extent possible.  Specific information and 
the general guidelines for remote participation by members of the public body and/or parties with a 
right and/or requirement to attend this meeting can be found on the Town website.
