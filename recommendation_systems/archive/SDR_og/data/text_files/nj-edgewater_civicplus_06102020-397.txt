Borough of Edgewater adopted a resolution authorizing the Edgewater Planning Board to 
undertake a preliminary investigation of property identified as follows:
Block 76, Lots 1 and 5
-
615 River Road
as identified on the Official Tax Map of the Borough of Edgewater (the "Study Area") to determine if the 
properties qualify as an area in need of redevelopment. The study area is bounded by River Road to the 
west, Block 75, Lots 2.01 and 4 to the north, the Hudson River to the east and Block 81, Lots 1 and 2 to

and June 1, 2020 at which time the hearing was adjourned for additional testimony. The hearing will 
resume on June 10, 2020 at 7:00 p.m. As a result of the COVID-19 pandemic, the Borough Hall is 
closed to the public, so the public hearing shall be continued via the virtual Zoom platform.
