The purpose of this meeting is to hear interested parties and citizens on a request by the
applicant, Dustin Burton, Renaissance Infrastructure Consulting, on behalf of the owner, Ronald
Mather, Mather Companies, to approve a Rezoning (RZ-05-20-7318) to amend the current
Planned Unit Development (PUD) and Restricted Overlay (R-O) on the subject property that is
currently zoned and proposed to remain zoned “RC/PUD/R-O” (Regional Commercial/Planned
Unit Development/Restricted Overlay) and to approve an Amended Planned Unit Development
Concept Plan (PUDC-05-20-7319) on a property generally located at the northwest corner of NE
Jefferson Street and NE Adams Dairy Road. The applicant is requesting approval of a multi-
building residential warehouse self-storage facility totaling approximately 48,000-sf. Interested
parties may view the application materials on file by submitting a request via email to
ComDevLegal@bluespringsgov.com or calling (816) 228-0207 for more information. The site is
legally described as follows:
All of Lot 2, Jefferson Corner a subdivision of land in the city of Blue Springs, Jackson County, 
Missouri according to the recorded plat thereof
