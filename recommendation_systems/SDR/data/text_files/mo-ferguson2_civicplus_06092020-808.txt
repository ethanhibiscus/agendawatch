CITY OF FERGUSON 
NOTICE OF PUBLIC HEARING 
 
In accordance with the requirements of Chapter 49 Zoning, of the City of 
Ferguson, Missouri, notice is hereby given by the Council of the City of Ferguson 
that a public hearing will be held on Tuesday, June 9th, 2020 at 7:00 p.m. in the 
Council Chamber at the Ferguson City Hall, 110 Church Street. Said hearing will 
be for the purpose of considering a Special Use Permit to operate a beauty salon 
located at 195 N. Florissant Road. The parcel is currently zoned DT-4, Downtown 
Commercial. Copies of the proposal are on file in the office of the City Clerk, at 
110 Church Street, Ferguson, Missouri, and are available for public review in that 
office during the normal business hours of 8:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. 
 
All persons interested may appear and be heard at that meeting.  
 
Anyone with special needs should contact the City Clerk at least 5 days before 
said Public Hearing by calling (314) 524-5152.
