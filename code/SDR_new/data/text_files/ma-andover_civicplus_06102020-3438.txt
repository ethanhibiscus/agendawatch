Town Clerk Date Stamp 
Board/Committee Name: 
Andover School Committee Negotiations Subcommittee for AEA Unit A 
Date:

Time of Meeting: 
 
3:30PM Open Session Immediately followed by Executive Session  
Location: 
Virtual Meeting – Via Remote Access 
Signature 
 
 
  
 
AGENDA 
 
1. Call to Order 
2. Open Session 
3. Motion to meet in executive session pursuant to M.G.L. chapter 30A, sec. 21(a) for the following purposes: 
a. Purpose (3): to discuss strategy with respect to and in preparation for collective bargaining with the 
Andover Educational Association (Unit A-Teachers) because an open discussion may have a detrimental 
effect on the bargaining position of the subcommittee, and not to return to Open Session. 
b. Purpose (7) to review and approve executive session minutes of the Andover School Committee 
Negotiations Subcommittee’s executive session meetings.   
c. Purpose (2): to conduct collective bargaining session with the Andover Educational Associations-Unit A. 
 EXECUTIVE SESSION  
1. Discussion of strategy with respect to and in preparation for collective bargaining with the Andover 
Educational Association (Unit A-Teachers) pursuant to MGL c. 30A section 21(a) purpose (3). 
2. Review and approve executive session minutes of the Andover School Committee Negotiations 
Subcommittee’s executive session meetings.   
3. To conduct a collective bargaining session with the Andover Educational Association-Unit A pursuant to 
MGL c.30A section 21(2) purpose (2). 
4. Adjournment 
 
 
 
 
Pursuant to Governor Baker’s March 12, 2020 Order Suspending Certain Provisions of the Open Meeting Law, 
and the Governor’s March 15, 2020 Order imposing strict limitation on the number of people that may gather 
in one place, this meeting of the Andover School Committee Negotiating Subcommittee will be conducted via 
remote participation to the greatest extent possible. Specific information and the general guidelines for remote 
participation by members of the public body and/or parties with a right and/or requirement to attend this 
meeting can be found on the Town website. 
 
 
To the best of our knowledge, this is the SC posted agenda for the upcoming meeting.  
However, other agenda items may arise, therefore the SC reserves the right to discuss additional agenda items if merited.
