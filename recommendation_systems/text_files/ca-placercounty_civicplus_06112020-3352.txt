at https://www.placer.ca.gov/2491/Municipal-Advisory-Councils-MACs.  Placer County is committed to ensuring that persons 
with disabilities are provided the resources to participate fully in public meeting. If you require disability-related modifications or

A. Workforce Housing Preservation Program 
Placer County staff will describe a proposed deed restriction program to preserve 
existing housing stock for the workforce to serve the tourism and local 
economies and sustain viable year‐round communities. 
Presenter: Emily Setzer, Placer County CEO’s Office

B. Potential Tahoe Basin Lodging Incentive Program 
County Executive Office and Economic Development staff will present progress on 
creating a TOT rebate program to incentivize lodging renovation and development 
in the Tahoe basin, including feedback from business and developer stakeholders 
and concept options being prepared for consideration by the Board of Supervisors. 
Presenters: Jennifer Merchant, Placer County CEO’s Office, and Paul Griffith, 
Placer County Economic Development  
 
C. Placer County Department of Public Works Pedestrian Improvements 
Placer County Department of Public Works will provide updates on upcoming 
pedestrian safety improvements in Eastern Placer County. 
Presenters: Peter Kraatz and Ryan Decker, Placer County Department of Public 
Works
