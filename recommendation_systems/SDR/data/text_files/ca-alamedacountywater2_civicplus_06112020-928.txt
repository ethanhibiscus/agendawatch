DETERMINATION OF APPROPRIATIONS LIMIT 
FOR FISCAL YEAR 2020/21 
 
 
 
 
California law (Government Code §7910) requires the governing body of each local 
jurisdiction, by resolution, to establish its appropriations limit for the following fiscal year 
pursuant to Article XIIIB of the California Constitution. The appropriations limit must be adopted 
at a regularly scheduled meeting or a noticed special meeting, and the documentation used in 
the determination must be made available to the public for fifteen (15) days prior to such 
meeting. 
 
 
Set out below is the methodology proposed to determine the Fiscal Year 2020/21 
appropriations limit for the Alameda County Water District. The FY 2020/21 appropriations limit 
will be considered at the June 11, 2020 Board of Directors meeting.
