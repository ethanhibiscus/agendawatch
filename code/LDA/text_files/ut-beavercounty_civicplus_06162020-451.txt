NOTICE OF PUBLIC HEARING 
 
NOTICE IS HEREBY GIVEN that the Beaver County Planning & Zoning Commission will 
hold a public hearing on Tuesday, June 16, 2020 at 6:30 pm at the Beaver County Administration 
Building at 105 East Center St., Beaver, Utah, at which time and place the commission will hear 
public comments concerning Milford Solar Phase II, LLC’s application for a Conditional Use 
Permit for the construction of a solar power generating facility, substation, transmission line, 
battery energy storage system and an operations/maintenance building in multiple sections of 
T26-27S, R10W in a Multiply Use (MU) District.  Complete details are on file in the Beaver 
County Clerk’s Office.  For questions please contact Kyle Blackner at 435-438-6483 or 
kblackner@beaver.utah.gov .
