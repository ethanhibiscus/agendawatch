Parks and Recreation Advisory Board Agenda
SPECIAL PARKS AND RECREATION ADVISORY BOARD  MEETING
WILL BE HELD ON JUNE 11, 2020 AT 5:30 P.M. 
The health, safety and well-being of our residents and businesses are a top priority for
the Parks and Recreation Advisory Board and Parks and Recreation Leadership.
As such, and consistent with and in support of the direction from Gov. Michelle
Lujan Grisham and the New Mexico Department of Health public health order,
the Special Parks and Recreation Advisory Board Meeting will be held via Zoom.
View meeting live on youtube.com/clctv20.
In an effort to seek public input, we ask that if you have comments/concerns/
questions, please e-mail:  parksandrecadmin@las-cruces.org
