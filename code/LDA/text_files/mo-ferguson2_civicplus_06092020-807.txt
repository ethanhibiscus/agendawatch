 
 
CITY OF FERGUSON, MISSOURI 
NOTICE OF PUBLIC HEARING 
 
In accordance with requirements of the Code of the City of Ferguson, Missouri, notice is 
hereby given by the Council of the City of Ferguson that a public hearing will be held on 
Tuesday, June 9, 2020 at 7:00 p.m. in the Council Chamber at Ferguson City Hall, 110 
Church Street.  Said hearing will be to present the proposed operating budget for Fiscal 
Year 2020 - 2021 and the proposed Five Year Capital Improvement Program for Fiscal 
Years 2021 - 2025.   
 
All interested citizens will have the opportunity to give written and oral comment.     
Copies of the proposed budget are on file in the office of the City Clerk at 110 Church 
Street, Ferguson, Missouri and are available for public review in that office during the 
normal business hours of 8:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. 
 
Anyone with special needs should contact the City Clerk at least 5 days before said 
Public Hearing by calling (314) 524-5152. 
 
All persons interested may appear and be heard at that meeting. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Octavia Pitman, City Clerk
