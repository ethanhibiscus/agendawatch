 
Plano Municipal Center is wheelchair accessible.  A sloped curb entry is available at the main entrance, facing 
Municipal/L Avenue, with specially marked parking spaces nearby.  Access and special parking are also available on 
the north side of the building.  Requests for special services must be received 48 hours prior to the meeting time by 
calling the Human Resources Department at 972-941-7115.
