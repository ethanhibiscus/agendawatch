6/11/20 Livingston County Planning Board Meeting 
Time: 7:00 PM 
 
 
Due to public health and safety concerns related to the COVID-19 Pandemic, 
the Livingston County Planning Board will not be meeting in person. 
In-person attendance will NOT be permitted. 
 
 
Per State and Federal bans on large gatherings and pursuant to Governor 
Cuomo’s Executive Order 202.1 issued on March 12, 2020, “suspending law 
allowing the attendance of meetings telephonically or other similar service”, 
the Livingston County Planning Board will conduct all meetings remotely using 
Zoom teleconferencing until further notice. 
 
 
All meetings will follow Roberts Rules of Order and will be recorded and later 
transcribed. 
 
 
Agendas are available per normal procedures on the County website 
www.livingstoncounty.us
