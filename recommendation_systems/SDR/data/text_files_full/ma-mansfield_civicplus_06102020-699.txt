TOWN OF MANSFIELD 
PLANNING BOARD 
NOTICE OF PUBLIC HEARING 
In accordance with Chapter 40A of the Massachusetts General Laws and the Mansfield 
Zoning By-Law, the Mansfield Planning Board will hold a public hearing on Wednesday, 
June 10, 2020 at 7:15 p.m. in the Planning Board’s meeting room at the Town Hall, Six Park 
Row, Mansfield, MA regarding Zoning By-Law amendments to be considered at the 2020 
Annual Town Meeting. The proposed amendments are as follows: 
 
*To amend the Zoning By-Law Section 3.6G, Wireless Towers, to add a new section (5), 
Small Wireless Facilities, to establish guidelines for permitting small wireless (5G) 
facilities; and 
 
*To amend the Zoning By-Law Section 230-3.5, Classification of business uses, A., Retail 
store, to exclude resident-only service uses and tenant rental storage space; and 
 
*To amend the Zoning By-Law Section 230-3.3, Classification of Residential Uses, D., 
Multiple residence, to require a special permit for the developments of 20 or more 
residential units; 
and to add a footnote referencing that requirement to: Section 230 Attachment 1:1, Schedule 
of Principal Use Regulations; to Attachment 1:3, Schedule of Principal Use Regulations; 
and to Section 230 Schedule 4.2B, Density and Dimensional Regulations, Business and 
Industrial Districts, Footnote 2; and 
 
*To amend the Zoning By-Law Section 230-5.10K, North Main Street Business Overlay 
District, to amend the density incentives in (6)[2], (6)[2](d) and (7)(a); and to delete (7)(d), 
which currently allows a developer to count on-street parking spaces toward parking 
requirements; and to amend (7)(d) to add a definition of first floor business and professional 
uses; and 
 
*To amend various portions of the Zoning By-Law Section 5.12, Mansfield Station 
Revitalization Overlay District, including parking, density and dimensional regulations, 
zoning incentives and density Bonuses. 
 
Complete descriptions of the proposed amendments are available at the Planning Department, 
located at Town Hall, Six Park Row, Mansfield, Massachusetts during regular business hours. 
This legal advertisement is also available at masspublicnotices.org.
