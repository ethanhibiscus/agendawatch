The purpose of this meeting is to hear interested parties and citizens on a request by the
applicant, David Williams, on behalf of the property owner, Luxe One, LLC, to approve a
Rezoning (RZ-04-20-7277) from “GB” (General Business) to “MF-18/PD” (High Density Multi-
Family/Planned Development) and to approve a Planned Development Concept Plan (PDC-04-
20-7278) on a property generally located north of NW North Ridge Drive approximately 195-ft.
west of NW 7 Highway. The applicant is requesting the approval of a four (4) story apartment
building with approximately 53 dwelling units. Interested parties may view the application
materials on file by submitting a request via email to ComDevLegal@bluespringsgov.com or
calling (816) 228-0207 for more information. The site is legally described as follows:
Lot 4, NORTH RIDGE LOTS 1-5, a subdivision in Blue Springs, Jackson County, Missouri.
